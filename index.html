History

In June 1997, Zhang Hongchao, a student at Henan University of Finance and Economics, was on the hunt for a part-time gig at a cool drinks kiosk to lighten the load on his family's finances. While working at the kiosk, he got inspired to start his own business.

With a loan from his grandma, he opened a shaved ice stall in his hometown of Zhengzhou, Henan, selling various types of shaved ice, ice cream, smoothies, and later adding pearl milk tea to the menu. However, his business journey wasn't a walk in the park, and he faced plenty of obstacles, eventually leading him to close down his first shop.

Not one to give up on his dreams, in 1999, Zhang opened a second ice stall, which he named Mixué Bingchéng (Hanzi: 蜜雪冰城), meaning “sweet snow palace.” He shifted his focus to ice cream products as soft-serve cone treats gained popularity in Zhengzhou since 2006 and ice cream prices soared.

To make his ice cream more affordable, Zhang reformulated the recipes, offering them at about 20 percent cheaper than similar products. His success in selling ice cream prompted him to venture into franchising in 2008.

Since the 2010s, Mixue has blossomed into its own company, expanding its franchise scheme to other regions throughout China. The company has research and development centers in other areas of Henan, as well as warehousing and logistics centers to streamline production costs.

In 2022, Mixue Bingcheng Co., Ltd. plans to go public on the Shenzhen Stock Exchange. Mixue’s ice cream business keeps growing and succeeding thanks to the dedication and hard work of Zhang Hongchao and his team.

Halal Certification

Mixue, a well-known brand in Indonesia, offers its flagship products of ice cream & tea. Mixue always prioritizes the halal certification of its products, which has been obtained from the Indonesian Council of Ulama’s Food, Drug, and Cosmetics Assessment Agency (LPPOM MUI).

The halal certification held by Mixue signifies that its products have undergone strict supervision and examination to ensure that the ingredients used and the manufacturing processes adhere to Islamic principles.

To obtain the halal certification from LPPOM MUI, Mixue must fulfill several requirements set by the organization. Some of the requirements include:

1. The raw materials used must come from halal sources and should not contain any forbidden substances such as pork or alcohol.

2. The production process must be carried out in a clean environment without any contamination of forbidden substances.

3. The entire production process must be closely monitored by trained personnel certified in halal practices.

4. All finished products must be labeled as halal and maintained in a clean and uncontaminated state.

Through its halal certification, Mixue is committed to providing high-quality and halal products to its consumers. This demonstrates that Mixue not only focuses on profitability but also considers the well-being and needs of its customers.

As discerning consumers, it is important for us to pay attention to the halal certification when choosing food and beverage products. By selecting products with halal certification, we can ensure that the products are safe and in accordance with our religious teachings.

Therefore, if you want to enjoy delicious and halal ice cream & tea, Mixue is the right choice. With its halal certification, Mixue guarantees the halal status of its products, providing confidence for consumers to enjoy its products peacefully and comfortably.
